Ravioli

Description

Mix flour, eggs, salt, and olive oil to make dough. Let it rest. For filling, combine ricotta, Parmesan, spinach, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Roll out dough, place filling, cover with another sheet, and seal. Boil ravioli for 3-4 minutes. Serve with tomato sauce or butter sage sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Make Dough
  2. Prepare Filling
  3. Assemble Ravioli
  4. Boil Ravioli
