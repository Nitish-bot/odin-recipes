Pizza


Description

Mix flour, yeast, warm water, sugar, salt, and olive oil to form dough. Let it rise. Prepare sauce with crushed tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, sugar, oregano, and basil. Roll out dough, spread sauce, and top with mozzarella, pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, and sausage. Bake at 475°F (250°C) for 10-15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare Dough
  2. Make Sauce
  3. Assemble
  4. Bake
